Mulligans

This is Mulligans, a YouTube golf channel Mitch and I run end-to-end - from the golf itself (despite how bad we are at it) to the filming, editing, thumbnails and distribution. Mulligans is built around long-form content, clipped down for short-form across Instagram, TikTok and YouTube Shorts.

Built from zero in February 2026, the channel has passed 100,000 views across 85 videos, with long-form retention consistently above 55%. We've recently begun working with commercial partners including Future Golf.
